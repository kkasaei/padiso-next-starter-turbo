## 5. Multi-Store SEO Architecture: Shopify Plus Best Practices

Let me walk you through bulletproof multi-store SEO setups I’ve seen succeed:

- Unique root domains for major markets (e.g., mybrand.co.uk, mybrand.de)
- Consistent URL structures (e.g., /collections/, /products/)
- Hreflang tags handled centrally or via an app—AI can audit/correct them at scale
- Canonical URL management to prevent duplicates
- Automated cross-store internal linking

Each layer can be monitored or managed by AI workflows for maximum control.

## 9. Meta Tags and Structured Data Optimization at Scale

Meta tags—titles and descriptions—still drive SEO. Manual updates? Out of the question for enterprise Shopify. Instead:

- AI writes unique, compelling meta tags per locale and per product
- Ensures character length and keyword fit for Google/Yandex/Baidu, etc.
- Auto-updates if product descriptions, prices, or inventory change

For schema, AI audits coverage and suggests new opportunities for FAQ/Product/Review markups. 

## 10. Scaling Internal Link Strategies in Multi-Store Shopify

Internal links are what glue your SEO together. With AI:

- Generate internal link maps for every store
- Identify orphaned pages (e.g., new products that aren’t linked anywhere)
- Suggest high-authority pages to redistribute link juice
- Automate contextually relevant in-content links (e.g., from blog to product collection)

This delivers rising SEO tide across every Shopify domain.
