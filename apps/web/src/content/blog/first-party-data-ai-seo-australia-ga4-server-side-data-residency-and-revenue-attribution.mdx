## 2. Unlocking GA4 Server-Side Tagging: What, How, and Why

GA4 server-side tagging lets you collect website and app data on your own Australian server before sending it to Google. Why does this matter?

  - It boosts data control—reducing data lost to ad blockers or browser privacy tools.
  - It future-proofs your tracking as third-party cookies die out.
  - It enables smarter consent handling (see Consent Mode below).

## 4. Consent Mode: Navigating Australian Privacy Laws

Google’s Consent Mode bridges privacy and measurement. It adjusts what is tracked based on a visitor’s consent.

  - Server-side GA4 tagging + Consent Mode = granular, lawful data collection in Australia.
  - Explicit consent triggers event-level tracking; declined consent collects only anonymous signals.

Real story: An Australian insurance brand used Consent Mode to maintain analytics reliability after cookie banners reduced their tracked sessions by 36%—and got it back up to 98% accuracy.
