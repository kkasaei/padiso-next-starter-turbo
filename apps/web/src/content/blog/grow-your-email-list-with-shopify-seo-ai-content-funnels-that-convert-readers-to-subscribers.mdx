## The SEO-to-Email Funnel

The funnel works like this:
1. **Shopper searches** for information related to your products
2. **Your blog post ranks** and captures the click
3. **Content provides genuine value** and builds trust
4. **Strategic CTA offers additional value** in exchange for email
5. **Email sequence nurtures** the subscriber toward purchase

Every step is amplified by AI: AI generates the content, optimises for search, and creates the lead magnets.

## AI-Generated Content for Each Funnel Stage

### Top of Funnel: Educational Blog Posts
AI generates informational content targeting "how to" and "what is" queries:
- Ranks for high-volume informational keywords
- Provides genuine value before asking for anything
- Establishes expertise and trust

### Middle of Funnel: Comparison and Guide Content
AI creates decision-stage content with embedded email capture:
- "Best [products] for [use case]" with "Get our full comparison PDF" CTA
- Buying guides with "Subscribe for personalised recommendations"
- Seasonal guides with "Get notified for the next [season] collection"

### Bottom of Funnel: Product-Adjacent Content
AI generates content that's one step from purchase:
- Sizing guides with "Email me my measurements"
- Style quizzes with "Send my results to my email"
- Wish list functionality with "Save your wish list via email"

## Technical Implementation on Shopify

### Blog Post CTAs
Embed email capture forms within blog content:
- After the introduction (for engaged readers)
- Mid-content (contextual offer relevant to the section)
- End of post (primary CTA with strongest value proposition)

### Exit-Intent Offers
Trigger email capture when organic visitors are about to leave:
- Content-specific offers (not generic "10% off" popups)
- "Loved this guide? Get our weekly [category] tips" 
- A/B test different value propositions per blog category

### Integration with Email Platforms
Connect to Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or Omnisend:
- Segment subscribers by which content they signed up from
- Trigger product recommendation sequences based on content interest
- Track revenue attribution from SEO-captured email subscribers

- **Time from subscription to first purchase** — how long does the nurture sequence take?

## FAQs

- **What's a good email capture rate from blog traffic?**
2-5% is good. 5-10% is excellent. Anything above 10% means your lead magnet is exceptionally well-matched to your content.

- **Should every blog post have an email capture CTA?**
Yes, but vary the approach. Some posts warrant a downloadable guide, others a newsletter signup, others a quiz.

- **How do I attribute revenue to SEO-captured email subscribers?**
Use UTM parameters on email links and track the original acquisition source in your email platform's subscriber properties.
