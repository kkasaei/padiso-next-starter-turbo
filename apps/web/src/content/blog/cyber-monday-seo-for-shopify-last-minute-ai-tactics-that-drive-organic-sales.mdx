## Tactic 1: Optimise Your Existing Pages (30 Minutes)

You don't need new pages—just update what you have:

### Update Meta Titles
Add "Cyber Monday" to your top collection page meta titles:
- Before: "Women's Running Shoes | Your Brand"
- After: "Women's Running Shoes — Cyber Monday Deals | Your Brand"

AI can generate updated meta titles for your top 50 pages in minutes.

### Update Meta Descriptions
Add urgency and deal messaging:
- "Shop our Cyber Monday deals — up to 50% off [category]. Free shipping on orders over $99. Ends midnight Tuesday."

### Add Sale Schema
Update Product schema with:
- `priceValidUntil` set to Cyber Monday end date
- Sale price in the `offers` property
- `availability` set to `InStock`

## Tactic 2: Create a Cyber Monday Hub Page (1 Hour)

Even at the last minute, a dedicated Cyber Monday page helps:
- Create `/pages/cyber-monday` with deal highlights
- Link to all sale collections
- Add email capture for deal notifications
- Include FAQ: "When do Cyber Monday deals end?" and "Do you offer free shipping on Cyber Monday?"
- Submit the URL for indexation immediately via Search Console

## Tactic 3: Publish Quick-Win Blog Content (2 Hours)

AI can generate these fast:
- "Our Best Cyber Monday Deals 2025" — roundup of your top offers
- "Cyber Monday vs Black Friday: What's Still on Sale" — captures comparison queries
- "Last Chance Deals: Cyber Monday [Category] Guide" — high urgency content

## Tactic 4: Internal Linking Blitz (30 Minutes)

Add prominent links to your Cyber Monday content:
- Homepage banner linking to Cyber Monday hub page
- Navigation bar announcement linking to deal collections
- Footer link to Cyber Monday deals
- Blog sidebar featuring Cyber Monday content

## Tactic 5: Optimise for "Cyber Monday + [Your Category]" (1 Hour)

These long-tail queries are less competitive:
- "Cyber Monday skincare deals Australia"
- "Cyber Monday running shoe deals"
- "Best Cyber Monday [product] deals 2025"

Create or update collection pages with these terms in headings and content.

## Post-Cyber Monday

After the sale:
- Don't delete Cyber Monday pages
- Update with "Sale ended — sign up for next year's deals"
- Keep the URLs live for next year
- Redirect deal-specific pages to evergreen alternatives

## FAQs

- **Is it really worth doing SEO for a one-day sale?**
"Cyber Monday deals" queries spike massively. Even capturing a small share drives significant revenue. Plus, the pages you create now serve you next year.

- **Can I rank for Cyber Monday queries in just a few days?**
For long-tail queries ("Cyber Monday [niche] deals"), yes. For "Cyber Monday deals" alone, you need established authority.
