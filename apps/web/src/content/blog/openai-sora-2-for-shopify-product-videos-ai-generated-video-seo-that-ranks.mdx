## The Video SEO Workflow for Shopify

Here's how to go from zero product videos to a fully optimized video library:

### 1. Identify Priority Products

Not every SKU needs a video on day one. Start with:

- **Top 100 revenue-generating products**
- **Products with high search volume but no video in SERPs**
- **Products with high return rates** (video reduces returns by showing products accurately)
- **New product launches** that need visibility fast

### 2. Generate Videos with Sora 2

For each product:

- **Input**: High-resolution product photos + detailed product description
- **Prompt**: Specify the video style—clean white background rotation, lifestyle context, or feature close-ups
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds is optimal for product videos
- **Output**: MP4 files ready for upload

### 3. Optimize for Video SEO

Raw video files need SEO treatment:

- **Video title**—include primary keyword + product name
- **Description**—200+ words with keywords, product specs, and purchase links
- **Thumbnail**—custom thumbnail with product image and text overlay
- **Schema markup**—VideoObject JSON-LD on the product page
- **Transcription**—even for short product videos, text transcripts help indexation
- **Video sitemap**—submit to Google Search Console

### 4. Deploy Across Channels

Upload each video to:

- **Shopify product page**—embedded as the hero or supplementary media
- **YouTube**—in your brand's shopping-enabled channel
- **Google Merchant Center**—attach to product feeds for Shopping results
- **Social media**—repurpose for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Pinterest Video Pins

## Technical Implementation for Shopify

Embedding AI-generated videos on Shopify without killing Core Web Vitals:

- **Lazy load all videos**—don't let them block initial page render
- **Use lightweight video players**—avoid heavy third-party embeds
- **Host on a CDN**—serve videos from Cloudflare or similar, not Shopify's native hosting
- **Compress intelligently**—target 720p for product pages, 1080p for YouTube
- **Implement poster images**—show a static frame until the user clicks to play

## Measuring Video SEO Impact

Track these metrics after deploying product videos:

- **Video rich result appearances** in Google Search Console
- **YouTube channel views and watch time** from shopping-related queries
- **Product page engagement**—time on page, bounce rate changes
- **Conversion rate impact**—do pages with videos convert better?
- **Return rate changes**—do accurate video representations reduce returns?
