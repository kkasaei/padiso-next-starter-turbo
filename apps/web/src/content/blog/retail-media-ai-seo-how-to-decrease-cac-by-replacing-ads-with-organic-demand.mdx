## 1. What Is Retail Media and Why Has It Exploded?

Retail media is the game-changer in digital advertising. Think Amazon Sponsored Products, Walmart Connect, Instacart Ads—these are networks on retailer sites where brands pay to get their products in front of shoppers. It’s exploded because shoppers go directly to these marketplaces ready to buy and because retailers are privy to first-party, high-intent data. But when everyone’s running to retail media, costs skyrocket, and your margins erode.

- Retail media now accounts for more than 20% of total digital ad spend in ecommerce.
- Competition is fierce—resulting in rising CPCs and shrinking ROAS.

## 2. Understanding CAC Reduction in a Paid-First World

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is the metric that can make or break your business. In a world where everyone launches paid campaigns, CAC can get out of control fast. But take heart—businesses that diversify into organic get breathing room for their budgets and better control over their acquisition machine.

- Paid CAC increases as competition grows
- Organic CAC = far lower ongoing cost if you build authority and rank

I’ve worked with plenty of ecommerce teams trapped by paid—until they built organic engines for sustainable growth.

## 3. Demystifying Organic Demand for Ecommerce

Organic demand is real customers searching for or discovering your product directly—without an ad click. Instead of paying a network for every visitor, you generate your own inbound momentum. This can come from search (Google, Bing, YouTube), marketplaces (Amazon SEO), editorial mentions, social sharing, and more.

- It's higher margin
- More likely to convert (authentic, high-intent traffic)
- Scales over time—unlike paid, where you start over every month

## 5. Paid vs. Organic Demand: Where Do You Invest?

Here’s the hard truth: Paid acquisition can deliver immediate results, but you pay for every click. Organic takes time, but delivers free traffic at scale once it’s working.

- Paid is best for rapid testing, launches, clearance
- Organic is crucial for long-term, efficient CAC reduction
- The smartest brands combine both—but prioritize transitioning spend to organic as fast as results allow

Think of it like planting fruit trees: Paid ads are like buying fruit at the store, while organic is growing your own orchard.

## 7. Tracking Blended ROAS: The New Standard

Blended ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) blends your spend across paid and organic channels. Instead of measuring paid in isolation, you’re looking at the real picture:

- Total revenue / (Paid media spend + organic investment costs)

This is the only way to see true efficiency as you move from ad dependence to AI SEO-driven organic growth.

## 14. Attribution Models: Moving Beyond Last-Click in Ecommerce SEO

Last-click attribution gave paid ads all the glory. But AI SEO reveals the complex journey. Consider:

- Linear attribution: Credit split across all touchpoints
- Position-based: Weight to first and last
- Algorithmic: Use AI to assign value based on what truly drives conversion

Brands that adopt blended attribution understand full-funnel impact and make better budget decisions.
