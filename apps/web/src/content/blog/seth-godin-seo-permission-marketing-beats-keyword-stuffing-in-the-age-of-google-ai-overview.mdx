## How Google AI Overview (SGE) Changes the SEO Game

Google’s Search Generative Experience (SGE) and Overviews are rewriting the rules. AI overviews pull what they think is the ‘best’ answer from billions of pages.

- SGE values context, depth, authority, and clarity over keyword density.
- It analyzes sentiment (are you helpful or salesy?)
- It highlights **trusted, original voices**.

If you’re still optimizing for “exact match” phrases, you’re invisible in SGE. Adapt by earning permission and becoming an authority in your niche.

## What Is AI-Native SEO and Why Is It Critical Now?

AI-native SEO is creating content for both humans and algorithms. The aim is to feed large language models (LLMs) with information that matches how users ask questions and make decisions.

- Focus on topics, not just keywords.
- Provide E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust).
- Optimize for semantic search and AI citation.

Brands adopting this now will own tomorrow’s search. Update your playbook, or risk disappearing.
