## 1. What is Programmatic SEO — And Why Does Everyone Mention Neil Patel?

In the SEO world, "programmatic SEO" refers to creating and scaling hundreds or thousands of web pages—often using data and automation—to target long-tail keywords. Neil Patel helped popularize this approach for mainstream marketers. He advocated that instead of just writing one blog per keyword, you can launch pages for every variation—cities, products, questions—by leveraging automation and templating.

Neil’s strategies focus on combining high-value keyword research, smart site structure, and lots of optimized, unique pages to own every possible search variation. He uses tools, but emphasizes quality, originality, and manual oversight.
