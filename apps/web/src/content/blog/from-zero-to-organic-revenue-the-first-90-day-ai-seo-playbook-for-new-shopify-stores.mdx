## Why 90 Days (and Not 6-12 Months)?

Traditional SEO wisdom says organic results take 6-12 months. That was true when everything was manual. AI compresses the timeline because:

- **Content production speed** — AI generates in hours what takes humans weeks
- **Technical fixes** — AI identifies and resolves issues in days, not sprints
- **Data-driven decisions** — AI spots opportunities instantly from competitor and search data
- **Scale** — AI handles 100 product pages as easily as 10

## Days 1-7: Technical Foundation

### Day 1-2: Shopify SEO Audit
Run a complete technical audit:
- Verify SSL is active
- Check robots.txt and sitemap.xml
- Ensure all pages are indexable
- Test page speed on mobile
- Verify Google Search Console is connected

### Day 3-4: Fix Critical Issues
Address blocking problems first:
- Fix any noindex tags on important pages
- Resolve duplicate content issues
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Set up Google Analytics 4

### Day 5-7: Schema Markup
Deploy structured data:
- Product schema on all product pages
- Organization schema on homepage
- BreadcrumbList schema site-wide
- Validate with Google's Rich Results Test

## Days 8-21: Content Foundation

### Week 2: Product Content
- Generate unique descriptions for your top 50 products (AI-powered)
- Optimize meta titles and descriptions for every product page
- Add alt text to all product images
- Create FAQ content for top product categories

### Week 3: Collection Content
- Write 800-1,200 word buying guides for your top 10 collections
- Optimize collection meta titles and descriptions
- Add internal links from collections to key products
- Implement collection-specific FAQ sections

## Days 22-45: Content Expansion

### Week 4-5: Blog Launch
- Create a topical map with 30-50 blog post ideas targeting non-brand queries
- Publish 3 pillar posts (2,000-3,000 words each) on your core topics
- Publish 10 supporting posts (1,500-2,000 words each) linking to pillar content
- Set up internal linking between blog posts and relevant products/collections

### Week 6: Link Foundation
- Submit your store to relevant business directories
- Create profiles on industry-specific platforms
- Reach out to 10 complementary brands for potential collaborations
- Guest post on 2-3 industry blogs

## Days 46-70: Optimization

### Week 7-8: Data-Driven Optimization
- Review Search Console data from the first 6 weeks
- Identify keywords where you're ranking positions 5-20 (strike distance)
- Optimize pages for these keywords with improved content and internal links
- Generate additional blog content targeting emerging keyword opportunities

### Week 9-10: Conversion Optimization
- Add trust signals to product pages (reviews, certifications, guarantees)
- Optimize product page layout for mobile conversion
- A/B test meta titles on your highest-impression pages
- Improve site speed based on Core Web Vitals data

## Days 71-90: Scale and Measure

### Week 11-12: Scale Content
- Expand product descriptions to remaining catalog
- Publish 10 more blog posts targeting keywords from Search Console data
- Create comparison and "best of" content for high-intent queries
- Build out FAQ schema across all major page types

### Week 13: Measure and Plan
- Compile 90-day SEO report with traffic, ranking, and revenue data
- Identify top-performing content and strategies to double down on
- Set quarterly goals based on first 90 days of data
- Plan the next 90-day sprint
