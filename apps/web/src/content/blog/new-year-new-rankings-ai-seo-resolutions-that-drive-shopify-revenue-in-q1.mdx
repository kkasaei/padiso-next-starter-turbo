## Why Q1 Is the Best Time to Start SEO

January offers unique SEO advantages:
- **New budget allocation** — fresh marketing budget to invest in organic
- **Post-holiday data** — November/December performance data reveals opportunities
- **New Year search spikes** — "new year" queries surge across every category
- **Reduced ad competition** — many brands pull back on ads in January, making organic even more valuable
- **Compounding timeline** — start in January, see results by April

## Resolution 1: Fix Your Technical Foundation (Week 1-2)

Before anything else, ensure your store's technical SEO is solid:
- Run a complete AI-powered SEO audit
- Fix all critical issues (noindex errors, broken links, missing sitemaps)
- Optimise Core Web Vitals
- Deploy schema markup across all page types
- Verify Search Console is properly configured

## Resolution 2: Optimise Your Product Catalog (Week 2-4)

Your products are your revenue pages:
- Generate unique descriptions for every product using AI
- Optimise meta titles and descriptions across the catalog
- Add alt text to all product images
- Create FAQ content for top product categories
- Implement comprehensive Product schema

## Resolution 3: Build Your Content Engine (Month 2)

Start the content flywheel:
- Create a topical map with 50-100 blog post ideas
- Publish 10-15 blog posts in February
- Create buying guides for your top collections
- Build "best of" and comparison content for high-intent queries
- Set a sustainable publishing cadence (3-5 posts per week)

## Resolution 4: Master Internal Linking (Month 2-3)

Connect your content ecosystem:
- Link blog posts to relevant products and collections
- Add cross-sell and related product content to PDPs
- Build collection-to-collection navigation
- Eliminate orphan pages
- Use AI embeddings for semantically intelligent link suggestions

## Resolution 5: Measure and Iterate (Ongoing)

Data-driven improvement:
- Set up weekly organic traffic and revenue tracking
- Monitor keyword ranking movements
- Track content performance by topic and format
- Identify winning patterns and double down
- Kill underperforming tactics early
