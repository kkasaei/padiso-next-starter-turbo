## Skill #1: Product Description Generator

**What it does**: Generates unique, SEO-optimized product descriptions from product data.

**Why it matters**: Most Shopify stores have hundreds or thousands of products with thin, generic, or missing descriptions. This skill fills the gap at scale.

**Key components**:
- Brand voice guidelines baked into the instructions
- SEO keyword integration rules
- Structure template (hook, benefits, details, social proof)
- Australian English spelling enforcement
- Length guidelines per product category (150 words for accessories, 300 for electronics)

**Impact**: 80% faster product content production. Stores with rich, unique descriptions rank higher and convert better—we typically see a 12-18% conversion rate improvement on pages with AI-optimized descriptions.

## Skill #2: Collection Page Content Writer

**What it does**: Generates editorial buying guide content for Shopify collection pages.

**Why it matters**: Collection pages target high-volume category keywords but most stores leave them as empty product grids. This skill turns every collection into a ranking-ready landing page.

**Key components**:
- 800-1,500 word buying guide structure
- Integration with product data from the collection
- FAQ generation (5-7 questions per collection)
- Internal linking recommendations
- Seasonal awareness (adjusts content for current season)

**Impact**: Collection pages with editorial content rank 40-60% higher for category keywords within 4-6 weeks.

## Skill #3: Meta Tag Optimizer

**What it does**: Generates click-optimized meta titles and descriptions with A/B test variants.

**Why it matters**: Meta tags are the first impression in search results. A 5% CTR improvement across your entire store compounds into significant traffic gains.

**Key components**:
- 60-character title limit enforcement
- 155-character description limit enforcement
- Primary keyword placement rules
- 3 variant generation for A/B testing
- Click trigger inclusion (numbers, years, benefits)

**Impact**: Average 8-15% CTR improvement across optimized pages. At scale, this translates directly to more organic traffic without any ranking changes.

## Skill #4: Blog Post Writer

**What it does**: Produces complete, SEO-optimized blog posts from a topic and keywords.

**Why it matters**: Your blog captures non-brand search traffic—the 80-90% of your addressable market where shoppers don't know your brand yet.

**Key components**:
- 2,000-3,000 word long-form content
- Keyword integration and heading optimization
- Internal linking to products and collections
- FAQ section with schema-ready structure
- CTA placement throughout the content

**Impact**: Stores publishing 8-12 AI-generated blog posts per month see 200-400% non-brand organic traffic growth within 90 days.

## Skill #5: Schema Markup Generator

**What it does**: Produces complete JSON-LD structured data for products, collections, FAQs, and articles.

**Why it matters**: Schema markup enables rich results in Google—star ratings, prices, FAQ dropdowns—that dramatically increase CTR and visibility.

**Key components**:
- Product schema with material, color, brand, availability
- AggregateRating from review data
- FAQPage schema from FAQ content
- BreadcrumbList schema
- Google compliance validation

**Impact**: 20-35% increase in rich result appearances within 3-4 weeks. Rich results consistently show 25-30% higher CTR than standard results.

## Skill #6: Email Campaign Writer

**What it does**: Generates email copy for campaigns, sequences, and automations tailored to your brand.

**Why it matters**: Email remains the highest-ROI marketing channel for ecommerce. But writing engaging emails for every campaign, segment, and product is time-consuming.

**Key components**:
- Subject line generation (10 variants ranked by predicted open rate)
- Pre-header text optimization
- Body copy in brand voice
- Product recommendations based on segment
- CTA optimization per email type (cart abandonment, welcome, winback, promotional)

**Impact**: Brands using AI-generated email copy see 15-25% improvement in open rates (better subject lines) and 10-20% improvement in click rates (more relevant content).

## Skill #7: Social Media Content Creator

**What it does**: Generates platform-specific social media posts for product promotion, brand storytelling, and engagement.

**Why it matters**: Maintaining consistent social presence across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Pinterest requires constant content production.

**Key components**:
- Platform-specific formatting (caption length, hashtag strategy, CTA style)
- Product photography caption writing
- Story/Reel script generation
- Content calendar suggestions
- Trend awareness and seasonal relevance

**Impact**: 3-5x increase in social posting frequency with consistent brand voice. Typically leads to 30-50% growth in social engagement.

## Skill #8: Competitor Analysis Reporter

**What it does**: Analyzes competitor websites and produces strategic intelligence reports.

**Why it matters**: Knowing what competitors are doing—and what they're missing—is essential for strategic marketing decisions.

**Key components**:
- Content strategy analysis
- SEO visibility comparison
- Pricing and positioning intelligence
- Content gap identification
- Actionable recommendations prioritized by impact

**Impact**: Identifies 10-15 content gaps per competitor analysis. Filling these gaps with your own content captures untapped search demand.

## Skill #9: Performance Insight Generator

**What it does**: Transforms raw analytics data into actionable marketing insights and recommendations.

**Why it matters**: Most marketing teams drown in data but starve for insights. This skill bridges the gap between data and action.

**Key components**:
- Organic traffic trend analysis
- Keyword ranking movement interpretation
- Revenue attribution insights
- Anomaly detection and explanation
- Prioritized action recommendations

**Impact**: Reduces reporting time by 80% and ensures the team focuses on the highest-impact opportunities every week.

## Skill #10: Customer Review Analyzer

**What it does**: Analyzes customer reviews to extract marketing insights, content ideas, and product improvement opportunities.

**Why it matters**: Your customers' words are your best marketing copy. Reviews contain the exact language, benefits, and objections your marketing should address.

**Key components**:
- Sentiment analysis across all reviews
- Common praise extraction (for marketing copy)
- Common complaints identification (for product and messaging improvement)
- Keyword extraction (what words do customers use to describe your products?)
- Content idea generation (what questions do reviewers answer?)

**Impact**: Marketing copy that uses customer language converts 20-30% better than internally generated messaging. Review analysis also surfaces product issues before they become PR problems.
