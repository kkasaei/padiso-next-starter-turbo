## The Evergreen Event Page Strategy

Create permanent event pages that you update annually:
- `/sales/eofy` — End of Financial Year sale hub
- `/sales/click-frenzy` — Click Frenzy deals page
- `/sales/boxing-day` — Boxing Day sale hub

Benefits of evergreen URLs:
- Accumulate backlinks and authority year after year
- Google learns to rank them for seasonal queries
- Less work each year (update content, not recreate pages)
- Compete with established retailers who've had these pages for years

## FAQs

- **How early should I prepare seasonal SEO content?**
4-6 weeks before the event. Google needs time to crawl, index, and rank new content.

- **Should I create content for every Australian shopping event?**
Focus on events relevant to your category. A fashion store should prioritise Click Frenzy and Boxing Day. An office supply store should prioritise EOFY.

- **Can I rank for "EOFY" queries as a small Shopify store?**
Yes—EOFY has less competition than Black Friday or Boxing Day. Long-tail queries like "EOFY [specific product] deals" are very accessible.
