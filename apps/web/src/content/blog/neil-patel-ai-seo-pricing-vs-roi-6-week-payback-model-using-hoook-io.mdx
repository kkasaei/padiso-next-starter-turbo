## 5. Breaking Down CAC and LTV for AI SEO Campaigns

You can’t fully evaluate SEO ROI without looking at your **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) and **LTV** (Lifetime Value).

Here’s how I recommend calculating:

  - **CAC = Total SEO Spend / Number of New Customers from SEO**
  - **LTV = Avg. Revenue Per Customer x Gross Margin x Retention Period**

When AI SEO reduces labor costs or generates conversions on autopilot, your CAC drops and LTV/CAC ratio rises, making your investment compelling.
