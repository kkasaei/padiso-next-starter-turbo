## 3. Hreflang: The Foundation of International Shopify SEO

Hreflang is like a signpost for Google—it tells search engines which version of your page to show in which language or country. Screwing up hreflang is the #1 cause of lost global rankings. Manual fixes work for five pages, but not 5,000.

- Each Shopify Market needs a proper hreflang mapping to its own URL and language-geo pairing.
- AI automation scans for missing or conflicting tags and applies corrections instantly.

Without strong hreflang, it’s chaos: US listings in France, Australian pages outranking your UK site, etc.

## 5. Avoiding Content Cannibalization Across Global Shopify Markets

Content cannibalization happens when multiple pages target the same keyword—Google gets confused, and your rankings die. International stores are at high risk because localized variants often use similar keywords.

- Smart AI will analyze on-page content, spot keyword overlap, and suggest unique title/meta tweaks for each region.
- Proper hreflang and canonical tags are your best armor.

## 7. Choosing the Right International Domain Structure on Shopify

Subdomains, subfolders, or ccTLDs? AI can evaluate your current site and recommend best-fit architecture for SEO:

- Subfolders (e.g. /fr/): Fast, SEO-friendly, no new domain needed.
- Subdomains (fr.domain.com): Good for regional silos.
- ccTLDs (domain.fr): Best for strong local trust, but resource intensive.

AI also helps with bulk redirects, canonicalization, and retrofitting old structures for new markets.
