## 1. What Did Seth Godin Mean by “Tiny Audiences”?

I’ve followed Seth Godin’s advice for years. He posits that you don’t need everyone; you just need the right “tribe.” Tiny audiences are micro-communities united by a clear intent or need. Godin famously said, “Find the smallest viable audience, delight them, and they’ll tell others.” It’s not about vanity traffic—it's about serving a hyper-relevant group that cares.

- Highly targeted with specific pain points.
- More likely to convert than broad anonymous visitors.
- Foster loyalty and word-of-mouth referrals.

## 3. How High-ROI Intent Pages Work

Intent pages are built for people ready to act, not just browse. That’s the magic. These pages are not blog posts—they’re laser-focused landing spots with precise answers, calls-to-action, and friction-free conversion paths. They turn micro-searches (the long-tail) into measurable revenue lifts.

- Answer intent-based queries (“best [tool] for [problem]”)
- Offer comparison tables, use-cases, or ROI calculators
- Always include zero-friction conversion options

Most marketers chase big keywords. I don’t. Long-tail searchers know what they want and are ready to buy. Tiny audiences are best found at the end of the long-tail: searches that signal real purchase or sign-up intent.

- Less competition, higher quality leads

## 5. What is MVA (Minimum Viable Audience) and Why It Matters

The MVA concept—hugely championed by Seth Godin—asks: “What’s the smallest group you can serve and still be successful?” Identifying your MVA means:

- Focusing messaging to one clear niche
- Building tailored content with direct CTAs
- Delivering results so good this audience can’t ignore you

## 8. How to Identify Your Most Profitable Tiny Audiences

## 9. The Anatomy of a High-ROI Intent Page

High-ROI intent pages share a set of features:

- Headline that matches searcher’s exact query
- Immediate value proposition—why this solution is perfect
- Trust signals: testimonials, logos, use-case examples
- Short forms or embedded chatbots for easy conversion
- Clear next steps: demo, pricing, free trial

Remember, clarity beats cleverness here.
