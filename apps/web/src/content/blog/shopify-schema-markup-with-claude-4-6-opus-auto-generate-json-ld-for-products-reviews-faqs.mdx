## Common Schema Mistakes on Shopify

- **Self-referencing reviews**—Google penalizes schema for reviews you wrote about yourself
- **Incorrect price format**—always include currency code, not just symbols
- **Missing availability**—InStock, OutOfStock, PreOrder must be accurate
- **Stale data**—schema must match visible page content exactly
- **Over-marking**—don't apply FAQ schema to content that isn't actually FAQs
