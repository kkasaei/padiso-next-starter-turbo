## 4. E-E-A-T: Elevating Authority with Authenticity

I’ve tested it: content that demonstrates E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust) gets top rankings.

Google’s AI Overview now rewards E-E-A-T, so let’s move beyond generic advice: back up claims with stories, testimonials, and real results.
