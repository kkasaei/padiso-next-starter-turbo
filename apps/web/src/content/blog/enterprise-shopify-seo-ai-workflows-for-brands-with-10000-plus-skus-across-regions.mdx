## The Enterprise SEO Scale Problem

At enterprise scale, the numbers are staggering:
- **10,000+ product pages** needing unique descriptions and meta tags
- **500+ collection pages** requiring editorial content
- **Multiple locales** with separate content needs per region
- **Daily catalog changes** — new products, price updates, discontinued items
- **Thousands of schema objects** requiring accuracy and compliance

Manual processes that work for 200 products break completely at 10,000.

## AI Workflow Architecture for Enterprise

### Workflow 1: New Product Pipeline
**Trigger**: Product created in Shopify
**Steps**:
1. AI generates product description from attributes and images
2. AI creates meta title and description
3. AI generates Product JSON-LD schema
4. AI suggests internal linking targets
5. Content publishes to store
6. Sitemap updates automatically
7. Monitoring begins for indexation and ranking

**Human involvement**: Review and approve (batch review of 50-100 products at a time)

### Workflow 2: Catalog Optimisation Sweep
**Trigger**: Weekly schedule
**Steps**:
1. AI scans all products for missing or thin descriptions
2. AI identifies pages with poor organic performance
3. AI generates improved content for underperforming pages
4. AI updates meta tags based on Search Console data
5. Changes queued for batch review and approval

### Workflow 3: Multi-Region Content Management
**Trigger**: Content approved in primary locale
**Steps**:
1. AI localises content for each target region
2. AI adjusts for local spelling, cultural references, and regulations
3. AI generates hreflang tags
4. Content publishes to regional storefronts
5. Regional sitemaps update

### Workflow 4: Continuous Monitoring
**Trigger**: Continuous
**Steps**:
1. AI monitors indexation status for all pages
2. AI tracks ranking changes for priority keywords
3. AI alerts team to significant drops or opportunities
4. AI generates weekly performance summaries
5. AI recommends optimisation priorities

## Quality at Scale

Enterprise AI SEO requires quality governance:
- **Automated quality scoring** for every piece of generated content
- **Sampling-based human review** — review 10% of output in detail
- **Brand voice validation** — automated checks against brand guidelines
- **Compliance scanning** — flag potentially non-compliant claims
- **A/B testing** — continuously test content variants for performance
