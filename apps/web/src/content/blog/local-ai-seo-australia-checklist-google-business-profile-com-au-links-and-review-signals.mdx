## 8. NAP Consistency for Local SEO

NAP = Name, Address, Phone Number. Here’s a story: I once helped an electrical contractor whose rankings lagged for months. Problem? Phone format varied from site to site. Tighten your NAP instantly:

  - Choose one format (e.g., 0412 345 678 or (02) 9876 5432)
  - Ensure **every** mention matches
  - AI auditing tools can auto-scan and spot inconsistencies instantly
