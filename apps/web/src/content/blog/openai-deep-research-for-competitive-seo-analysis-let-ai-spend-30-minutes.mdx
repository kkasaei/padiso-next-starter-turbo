## What Is OpenAI Deep Research?

Deep Research is OpenAI's autonomous research agent. Give it a question, and it:

- **Browses the web extensively**—reading dozens of pages in depth
- **Synthesizes findings**—compiles information into a structured report
- **Cites sources**—every claim links back to the original page
- **Spends 5-30 minutes** working autonomously on complex queries

For SEO, this means you can ask it to analyze a competitor's entire organic strategy and get a comprehensive report.

## The Competitive SEO Analysis Workflow

### Analysis 1: Competitor Content Strategy

**Prompt**: "Analyze [competitor URL]'s blog and content strategy. Identify their top-performing content topics, publishing frequency, content formats, average content length, and the keywords they appear to target. Compare with [your URL]."

**What you get**: A detailed report covering their content pillars, topic clusters, content gaps you can exploit, and their content production cadence.

### Analysis 2: Technical SEO Comparison

**Prompt**: "Compare the technical SEO implementation of [competitor URL] vs [your URL]. Analyze page speed, mobile usability, schema markup usage, site architecture, internal linking patterns, and Core Web Vitals."

**What you get**: A side-by-side technical comparison highlighting where you're ahead and where you need to catch up.

### Analysis 3: Backlink and Authority Analysis

**Prompt**: "Research [competitor URL]'s link building strategy. What types of sites link to them? What content earns the most links? Are there link building tactics visible from their public profile?"

**What you get**: Insights into their link acquisition patterns and tactics you can replicate or improve upon.

### Analysis 4: SERP Landscape Mapping

**Prompt**: "For the keyword '[target keyword]', analyze the top 10 ranking pages. What do they have in common? What content elements appear on all top-ranking pages? What's missing that could differentiate a new page?"

**What you get**: A blueprint for content that can compete with current top-ranking pages.

## Limitations of AI-Powered Competitive Analysis

Be aware of what Deep Research can and can't do:

**Can do:**
- Analyze publicly visible content and strategy
- Identify content gaps and technical differences
- Map SERP landscapes for specific keywords

**Can't do:**
- Access competitors' analytics data
- See their private keyword research
- Predict their future strategy
- Guarantee accurate traffic estimates

Use Deep Research as a starting point, then validate with SEO tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush) for quantitative data.
