## Why SEO Matters Before $1M

Most pre-$1M Shopify stores rely 80-100% on paid ads. The problem:
- Ad costs are rising 20-30% annually
- CAC increases as you exhaust warm audiences
- Revenue stops the moment you stop spending
- You're building on rented ground

SEO creates an owned traffic channel that compounds. Every dollar invested in organic content today pays dividends for years. Start now, and by the time you hit $1M, organic could be 20-30% of your traffic—dramatically improving unit economics.

## The Pre-Product-Market Fit SEO Strategy

Before you've found product-market fit, SEO serves a different purpose:

### 1. Market Research Through Search Data
Keyword research reveals what customers actually want:
- What queries have high volume in your category?
- What questions are potential customers asking?
- What language do they use to describe their problems?
- Where are the gaps in competitor content?

### 2. Content as Market Validation
Blog content tests market interest:
- Publish content about your product category
- See which topics drive the most traffic and engagement
- Use organic traffic patterns to validate product assumptions
- Identify the customer segments most interested in your niche

### 3. Foundation Building
Technical SEO work done now pays off later:
- Proper site structure from the start
- Schema markup on all pages
- Clean URL architecture
- Fast, optimised theme

## The Post-Product-Market Fit SEO Playbook

Once you've found what sells, scale organic aggressively:

### Month 1-2: Foundation
- Optimise your top 20 product pages (descriptions, meta tags, schema)
- Create 5 collection pages with editorial content
- Publish 10 blog posts targeting non-brand queries
- Submit sitemap and verify indexation

### Month 3-4: Expansion
- Expand to all product pages
- Create comparison and "best of" content
- Build FAQ content from customer questions
- Start collecting reviews for schema markup

### Month 5-6: Scale
- Publish 15-20 blog posts per month
- Create seasonal and trending content
- Expand to long-tail keyword targets
- Build internal linking architecture

## FAQs

- **Should I focus on SEO or ads first?**
Both—but allocate 80% to ads and 20% to SEO initially. As organic traffic grows, gradually shift the balance.

- **How long until SEO contributes meaningful revenue?**
3-6 months for initial revenue contribution. 6-12 months for significant impact (10-20% of total revenue).

- **What's the minimum SEO effort that makes a difference?**
Optimise your top 20 product pages and publish 2-4 blog posts per month. This minimal effort compounds over time.
