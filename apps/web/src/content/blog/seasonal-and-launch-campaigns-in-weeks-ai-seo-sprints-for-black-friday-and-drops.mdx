## 3. What is Inventory-Based SEO for Campaigns?

Traditional SEO plans ignore what's actually available to buy on your site. Inventory-based SEO flips this model:

- **Connects live inventory** to keyword and page opportunity
- Prioritizes **collections**, landing pages, and deals that have stock to sell
- Minimizes wasted SEO effort on OOS (out-of-stock) products

During Black Friday, this ensures your highest-stock items power your SEO wins.

## 15. Reducing Cannibalization Between Promo and Category Pages

If your promo and main catalog pages compete for the same keywords, you’re burning resources. Fix it by:

- Differentiating intent—promo pages for “deal” queries, catalog for “browse” queries
- Canonical tags and dynamic internal linking to guide Google
- AI can manage and update these relationships at scale

This keeps both page types visible, without undercutting your authority.
