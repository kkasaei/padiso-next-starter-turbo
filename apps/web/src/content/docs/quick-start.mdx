This guide walks you through generating your first AI visibility report and understanding the results.

## Step 1: Enter Your Domain

From the homepage or dashboard, enter your website domain (e.g., `example.com`) in the search field.

<Callout>
You don't need to include `https://` or `www.` - just the domain name is enough.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Generate Your Report

Click **"Get Free Report"** to start the analysis. SearchFit will:

1. Query multiple AI platforms about your domain
2. Analyze how your brand is perceived
3. Compile recommendations for improvement
4. Generate a comprehensive AEO score

This process typically takes 30-60 seconds.

## Step 3: Review Your Results

Your AEO report includes several key sections:

### Overall Score
A score from 0-100 indicating your current AI visibility. Higher scores mean better representation across AI platforms.

### AI Platform Breakdown
See how you perform on each platform:
- **ChatGPT** - OpenAI's conversational AI
- **Perplexity** - AI-powered search engine
- **Claude** - Anthropic's AI assistant
- **Gemini** - Google's AI platform

### Recommendations
Actionable suggestions to improve your AI visibility, prioritized by impact.

### Competitor Analysis
See how you compare to competitors in your industry.

## Step 4: Take Action

Based on your report, you can:

- **Download PDF** - Share the report with your team
- **Create Account** - Save reports and track progress over time
- **Implement Fixes** - Follow the recommendations to improve your score
