The AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Report is SearchFit's flagship analysis that measures how well your brand performs across AI platforms.

## What's in an AEO Report?

Your report includes comprehensive analysis across multiple dimensions:

### Overall AEO Score

A score from 0-100 representing your overall AI visibility:

| Score Range | Rating | Description |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| 80-100 | Excellent | Strong AI presence, frequently recommended |
| 60-79 | Good | Solid visibility with room for improvement |
| 40-59 | Fair | Moderate presence, significant opportunities |
| 20-39 | Poor | Limited AI visibility |
| 0-19 | Critical | Minimal or no AI presence |

### Platform Breakdown

Individual scores for each AI platform:

- **ChatGPT Score** - Performance in OpenAI's assistant
- **Perplexity Score** - Visibility in AI search
- **Claude Score** - Presence in Anthropic's assistant
- **Gemini Score** - Performance in Google's AI

## Report Sections

### Brand Awareness

Measures whether AI platforms know about your brand:

- **Recognition Rate** - How often AI recognizes your brand name
- **Information Accuracy** - Whether AI has correct information
- **Entity Definition** - How well AI understands what you do

### Recommendation Score

How likely AI is to recommend your brand:

- **Direct Recommendations** - When users ask for solutions
- **List Inclusions** - Appearing in "best of" lists
- **Comparison Mentions** - Being mentioned alongside competitors

### Content Citation

Whether AI platforms cite your content:

- **Citation Frequency** - How often your content is referenced
- **Source Authority** - How your site is perceived as a source
- **Topic Coverage** - Topics where you're cited

### Competitive Position

How you compare to competitors:

- **Market Position** - Where you rank vs competitors
- **Share of Voice** - Percentage of mentions in your category
- **Competitive Gaps** - Where competitors outperform you

## Recommendations

Each report includes prioritized recommendations:

### High Impact
Actions that will significantly improve your score:
- Fix critical accuracy issues
- Add missing structured data
- Create FAQ content for common queries

### Medium Impact
Improvements that will incrementally boost visibility:
- Enhance about page content
- Improve entity clarity
- Build authoritative backlinks

### Low Impact
Nice-to-have improvements:
- Minor content updates
- Additional schema markup
- Social profile optimization

## Understanding Metrics

### Mention Sentiment

How AI platforms discuss your brand:

- **Positive** - Favorable mentions, recommendations
- **Neutral** - Factual mentions without judgment
- **Negative** - Critical mentions, complaints
- **Mixed** - Combination of sentiments

### Accuracy Assessment

Whether AI information is correct:

- **Correct** - Information matches reality
- **Outdated** - Information was once correct
- **Incorrect** - Factually wrong information
- **Unknown** - Unable to verify

### Coverage Score

How comprehensively AI covers your brand:

- Services/products mentioned
- Locations covered
- Team members recognized
- History and background

## Report Comparisons

### Historical Comparison

Compare your current report to previous reports:

1. Go to **Reports** → **History**
2. Select two reports to compare
3. View score changes and trends
4. Identify improvements and regressions

### Competitor Comparison

Compare your report to competitors:

1. Add competitor domains
2. Generate reports for each
3. View side-by-side comparison
4. Identify competitive advantages

## Scheduling Reports

Set up automatic report generation:

1. Go to **Reports** → **Schedule**
2. Choose frequency (weekly, monthly)
3. Select domains to include
4. Configure delivery method
5. Add recipients for email delivery

## Best Practices

1. **Generate regularly** - Track progress over time
2. **Act on recommendations** - Implement suggested changes
3. **Monitor competitors** - Stay aware of market changes
4. **Share with stakeholders** - Keep team informed
5. **Celebrate wins** - Acknowledge improvements
