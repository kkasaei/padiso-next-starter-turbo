SearchFit's Content feature helps you create content that ranks well in both traditional search engines and AI-powered platforms.

## AI-Powered Content Generation

Our content tools leverage AI to help you create:

- **Blog posts** optimized for search and AI visibility
- **Product descriptions** that convert
- **Landing pages** designed for discovery
- **FAQ content** structured for AI answer engines

## Getting Started with Content

### Content Templates

Choose from pre-built templates:

1. Go to **Content** → **Create New**
2. Select a template type:
   - Blog Post
   - Product Page
   - Landing Page
   - FAQ Section
   - Comparison Article
3. Enter your topic and keywords
4. Generate and refine

### Custom Content

For more control:

1. Select **Custom Content**
2. Describe what you want to create
3. Specify tone, length, and style
4. Add keywords to target
5. Generate and edit

## AEO-Optimized Content

Content optimized for Answer Engine Optimization includes:

### Structured Data

Automatically generates:
- FAQ schema markup
- HowTo structured data
- Product schema
- Organization data

### Question-Based Sections

Formats content to answer common questions, making it easier for AI to extract and cite.

### Entity Optimization

Ensures your content:
- Clearly identifies your brand entity
- Links to authoritative sources
- Establishes topical authority

## Content Analysis

Before publishing, analyze your content:

### Readability Score
Ensures content is accessible and easy to understand.

### Keyword Coverage
Verifies target keywords are naturally included.

### AEO Score
Predicts how well the content will perform with AI platforms.

### Competitor Comparison
See how your content stacks up against competitors.

## Content Calendar

Plan and schedule your content:

1. Navigate to **Content** → **Calendar**
2. View upcoming content
3. Drag and drop to reschedule
4. Set publishing reminders

## Integration with CMS

Publish directly to your CMS:

- **WordPress** - Direct publishing and updating
- **Webflow** - Export formatted content
- **Shopify** - Product and blog content
- **Custom CMS** - API integration available
