Backlinks remain a crucial ranking factor for both SEO and increasingly for AI visibility. SearchFit helps you monitor and improve your backlink profile.

## Why Backlinks Matter for AEO

AI platforms consider authority signals when deciding which sources to cite and recommend. A strong backlink profile:

- Establishes your site as authoritative
- Increases likelihood of AI citations
- Improves trust signals across platforms
- Supports overall visibility scores

## Backlink Dashboard

Your backlink dashboard shows:

- **Total Backlinks** - All links pointing to your site
- **Referring Domains** - Unique domains linking to you
- **Domain Rating** - Overall link authority score
- **New vs Lost** - Link acquisition and loss tracking

## Monitoring Features

### New Backlink Alerts

Get notified when you earn new links:

1. Go to **Backlinks** → **Settings**
2. Enable **New Link Alerts**
3. Set minimum domain authority threshold
4. Choose notification method

### Lost Backlink Tracking

Monitor when links disappear:

- Automatic detection of removed links
- Context about why links may have been removed
- Suggestions for link reclamation

### Competitor Backlink Analysis

See where competitors get their links:

1. Add competitor domains
2. View their backlink profile
3. Identify link opportunities
4. Find common linking patterns

## Link Quality Assessment

Not all backlinks are equal. SearchFit evaluates:

### Authority Metrics
- Domain Rating (DR)
- Page Authority
- Trust Flow

### Relevance Signals
- Topical relevance
- Geographic relevance
- Industry alignment

### Risk Indicators
- Spam score
- Link farm detection
- Potential penalties

## Backlink Opportunities

SearchFit identifies link building opportunities:

### Unlinked Mentions
Finds sites mentioning your brand without linking.

### Competitor Gap Analysis
Identifies sites linking to competitors but not you.

### Broken Link Opportunities
Finds relevant broken links you could replace.

### Resource Page Prospects
Identifies resource pages in your niche.

## Link Building Outreach

Manage outreach campaigns:

1. Go to **Backlinks** → **Outreach**
2. View identified opportunities
3. Generate personalized outreach templates
4. Track email status and responses
5. Mark links as acquired

## Disavow Management

Handle toxic backlinks:

1. Review flagged links in **Backlinks** → **Toxic**
2. Investigate potentially harmful links
3. Add to disavow list if needed
4. Export disavow file for Search Console

## Reports

Generate backlink reports:

- **Profile Overview** - Complete backlink summary
- **New Links Report** - Recent acquisitions
- **Competitor Comparison** - Side-by-side analysis
- **Opportunity Report** - Link building prospects

## Best Practices

1. **Quality over quantity** - Focus on authoritative, relevant links
2. **Monitor regularly** - Check for new and lost links weekly
3. **Analyze competitors** - Learn from their link strategies
4. **Diversify sources** - Build links from varied domains
5. **Track ROI** - Measure impact on visibility scores
