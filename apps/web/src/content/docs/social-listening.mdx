Social Listening helps you track what people are saying about your brand across social media platforms and the broader web, providing insights that complement your AI visibility data.

## Why Social Listening Matters

Brand mentions across social and web platforms:

- Influence AI training data
- Affect brand perception in AI responses
- Provide early warning of reputation issues
- Identify customer feedback and trends

## Dashboard Overview

Your social listening dashboard shows:

- **Mention Volume** - Total mentions over time
- **Sentiment Analysis** - Positive/neutral/negative breakdown
- **Top Sources** - Where mentions originate
- **Trending Topics** - Associated themes
- **Influencer Activity** - High-profile mentions

## Sentiment Analysis

Automatic sentiment classification:

### Positive
Praise, recommendations, success stories

### Neutral
General mentions, news coverage, informational

### Negative
Complaints, criticism, issues

### Alerts
Configure alerts for sentiment shifts or spikes in negative mentions.

## Engagement Tools

2. Compare mention volume
3. Analyze sentiment differences
4. Identify market trends

## Best Practices

1. **Monitor daily** - Check your dashboard regularly
2. **Respond quickly** - Address negative mentions promptly
3. **Track trends** - Look for patterns over time
4. **Engage positively** - Build relationships with advocates
