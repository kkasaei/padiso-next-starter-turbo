After creating your account, follow these steps to configure SearchFit for your needs.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit the [sign up page](/auth/sign-up)
2. Enter your email and create a password, or sign in with Google
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

## Adding Your First Domain

Once logged in, add your primary domain:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** → **Domains**
2. Click **Add Domain**
3. Enter your domain name
4. Optionally verify ownership for additional features

### Domain Verification

Verifying domain ownership unlocks:
- Historical tracking data
- Automated monitoring
- Integration with Search Console
- Priority support

Verification methods:
- **DNS Record** - Add a TXT record to your DNS
- **HTML File** - Upload a verification file to your site
- **Meta Tag** - Add a meta tag to your homepage

## Configuring Notifications

Stay informed about your AI visibility:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose your notification preferences:
   - Email alerts for score changes
   - Weekly summary reports
   - Competitor movement alerts
   - New recommendation notifications

## Team Access

Invite team members to collaborate:

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email and select a role:
   - **Admin** - Full access to all features
   - **Editor** - Can run reports and make changes
   - **Viewer** - Read-only access to reports

## Billing Setup

Manage your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View your current plan and usage
3. Upgrade or modify your subscription
4. Update payment methods
