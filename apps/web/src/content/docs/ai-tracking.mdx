AI Tracking is SearchFit's core feature that monitors how AI platforms perceive and recommend your brand.

## How AI Tracking Works

SearchFit queries AI platforms with questions relevant to your industry and monitors:

- **Mention Frequency** - How often your brand is mentioned
- **Mention Context** - Whether mentions are positive, neutral, or need improvement
- **Accuracy** - Whether AI platforms have correct information about your business
- **Ranking** - Where you appear in AI-generated lists and recommendations

## Supported Platforms

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)

The most widely used AI assistant. We track:
- Brand mentions in conversational responses
- Accuracy of business information
- Recommendation patterns

### Perplexity

An AI-powered search engine gaining significant market share. We monitor:
- Appearance in search results
- Citation of your content
- Answer box inclusions

### Claude (Anthropic)

A thoughtful AI assistant known for accuracy. We track:
- Brand awareness and perception
- Factual accuracy of mentions
- Competitive positioning

### Gemini (Google)

Google's AI platform with deep integration into search. We monitor:
- AI Overview appearances
- Brand entity recognition
- Local business mentions

## Setting Up Tracking

### Automatic Tracking

Once you add a domain to SearchFit, basic tracking begins automatically:

1. Daily queries about your brand
2. Weekly comprehensive analysis
3. Monthly trend reports

### Custom Queries

Configure specific queries to track:

1. Go to **AI Tracking** â†’ **Custom Queries**
2. Click **Add Query**
3. Enter the question you want to monitor
4. Select which platforms to track
5. Set tracking frequency

Example queries:
- "What are the best [your industry] companies?"
- "Who provides [your service] in [your location]?"
- "What is [your brand name]?"

## Understanding Your Metrics

### Visibility Score

A composite score (0-100) based on:
- Mention frequency across platforms
- Mention quality and context
- Accuracy of information
- Competitive ranking

### Platform Scores

Individual scores for each AI platform, helping you identify where to focus optimization efforts.

### Trend Analysis

Track your visibility over time:
- Daily snapshots
- Weekly summaries
- Month-over-month comparisons
- Year-over-year trends

## Alerts and Notifications

Configure alerts for:

- **Score Changes** - Get notified when your visibility score changes significantly
- **New Mentions** - Alerts when AI platforms start mentioning your brand
- **Accuracy Issues** - Notifications when AI provides incorrect information
- **Competitor Movement** - Alerts when competitors gain or lose visibility

## Best Practices

1. **Track consistently** - Set up regular monitoring schedules
2. **Monitor competitors** - Add competitor domains for comparison
3. **Act on insights** - Use recommendations to improve visibility
4. **Verify accuracy** - Regularly check that AI has correct business info
