SearchFit offers a free AEO report for any domain, giving you instant insights into your AI visibility without creating an account.

## Getting Your Free Report

### Step 1: Enter Your Domain

Visit [SearchFit's homepage](/) and enter your domain in the search field.

<Callout>
No need to include `https://` or `www.` - just enter `yourdomain.com`
</Callout>

### Step 2: Wait for Analysis

Our system will:
- Query AI platforms about your domain
- Analyze responses and mentions
- Calculate your AEO score
- Generate recommendations

This typically takes 30-60 seconds.

### Step 3: View Results

Your free report includes:
- Overall AEO score
- Platform breakdown (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini)
- Top recommendations
- Competitor snapshot

## PDF Download

To download a PDF of your free report:

1. Complete your report generation
2. Click **Download PDF**
3. Enter your email address
4. Verify the reCAPTCHA
5. Receive download link via email

### Why Email Required?

We request your email to:
- Deliver the PDF link securely
- Notify you when the PDF is ready
- Provide follow-up tips (optional)

<Callout>
We respect your privacy. Unsubscribe from tips anytime, and we never share your email.
</Callout>

## Understanding Your Score

### Score Interpretation

| Score | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| 80+ | Excellent AI visibility - you're well-positioned |
| 60-79 | Good visibility with clear improvement areas |
| 40-59 | Moderate visibility - significant opportunity |
| 20-39 | Low visibility - action needed |
| 0-19 | Minimal AI presence - critical attention required |

### Platform Scores

Each platform score indicates:
- How well that AI knows your brand
- Whether information is accurate
- Likelihood of recommendations

## Free Report Limitations

The free report provides a snapshot but has limitations:

### Not Included
- Full mention analysis
- Historical comparisons
- Detailed competitor data
- Custom recommendations
- Ongoing monitoring
- API access

### Report Expiration
- Free reports are available for 7 days
- After 7 days, regenerate for fresh data
- Create an account to save reports permanently

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often can I generate free reports?

You can generate a free report once per domain per 24 hours.

### Is my data saved?

Free reports are stored temporarily (7 days) and then deleted. Create an account to save reports permanently.

### Can I share my free report?

Yes! Use the share button to copy a link. The link expires when the report does.

### Is the free report accurate?

Yes, free reports use the same analysis engine as paid reports. The difference is in the depth of detail provided.

### Do I need to verify my domain?

No, domain verification is not required for free reports. Verification is optional for account holders who want additional features.
